2B2T is a hostile vanilla overworld, overrun by PvP bots. The dimension is not a 1 to 1 recreation of 2B2T, but a combination of world downloads optimized for better exploration/combat in a smaller world size.

The 1000x1000 block radius around 0,0 is the true 2B2T spawn, with all of the obscenities removed. 

Outside of that radius however, the map is highly condensed to prevent excess bloat. Most bases can be found in the immediate area outside of spawn, rather than where their actual coords would be on 2B.
