The 1000x1000 block radius around 0,0 is the true 2B2T spawn, with all of the obscenities removed. 

You must escape from spawn to reach the outer chunks, where resources can be harvested. The 2B2T dimension is designed to be an all-in-one mining world, meaning all vanilla biome types can be found here, including overworld, nether, and end biomes.
