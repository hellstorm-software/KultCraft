The Modern Pearls Data Pack causes pearls to force load the chunk they are in, similarly to vanilla 1.20.1. However, it cannot recreate the effect of neighboring chunks being lazy loaded, which means remote stasis chambers still won’t work. To account for that, you have access to the remote fishing rod. 

To use the Remote Rod, craft a Redstone Transmitter, a coordinate card, and the Remote Rod. Placing the transmitter next to you stasis chamber, and then right clicking the transmitter while holding the coordinate card will link the two together. 
Right clicking with the remote rod in your hand will allow you to input the card into the rod, linking the rod to your stasis chamber. Left clicking with the rod will now activate the trapdoor, and trigger the stasis chamber.
